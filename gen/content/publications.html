Active Research Projects ======================================================================== - [**_Lightweaver_**:](https://github.com/Goobley/Lightweaver) A pythonic plane-parallel and 2D NLTE radiative transfer framework for the 21st century. Due to its flexibility _Lightweaver_ is growing tendrils in many directions at the moment, being used in flare, quiet sun, and prominence modelling. The numeric core is written in relatively optimised C++, currently designed for the single shared-memory systems most researchers have access to. - **Enabling Flare Atmospheric Diagnostics with Machine Learning**: This started with [**RADYNVERSION**](https://github.com/Goobley/Radynversion), but continues to be a very active topic, as we prepare for flare observations with unprecedented resolution over the coming solar cycle!
